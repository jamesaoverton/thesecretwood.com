About the Author - S.G. Overton

S.G. Overton has been a professional radio broadcaster for thirty years, 22 of those as host of his own morning show. Specializing in Drama at university, the professional theatre beckoned him, but radio satisfied a need to perform and a lifelong love of music. Even so, writing was, and still is, his first love. He now writes full-time with a number of short stories accepted for publication, and several works in progress in the thriller and science fiction genres.
